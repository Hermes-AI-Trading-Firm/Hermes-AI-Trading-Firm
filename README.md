## Rules

- Never claim profitability without a backtest.
- Never invent results.
- Never approve a strategy from optimization alone.
- Never delete rejected strategies.
- Never live trade without human approval.
- Always track why a strategy worked or failed.
- Always update the dashboard and AI Learning Brain.
- Always identify market regime performance.
- Always compare optimized results against baseline results.
- Always explain whether a strategy is robust or overfit.
