# Hermes AI Trading Firm — Workflow

## Strategy Lifecycle

```
Market Research
    → Strategy Factory
        → Quant Research
            → PineScript Development
                → TraderDev Backtesting
                    → Risk Review
                        → Market Regime Analysis
                            → Optimization Lab
                                → Walk-Forward Testing
                                    → Monte Carlo Testing
                                        → AI Learning Brain
                                            → Dashboard
                                                → Human Review
                                                    → Forward Testing
                                                        → Approved Strategy
                                                        OR
                                                        → Rejected Strategy
```

## Department Duties

See `agents/` for each department's prompt and responsibilities.

## Rejection Policy

Failed or dangerous strategies are archived under `research/rejected/` with a reason file. They are never deleted.

## Human Gate

No strategy moves to live trading without explicit human approval.
